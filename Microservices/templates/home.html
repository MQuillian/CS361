How to Create a Recipe

Creating a recipe is super easy and only requires a few clicks!

As you can see in the .gif below, begin by clicking the 'Create Recipe' button on the home screen.

Then, simply fill in the form with the name of your recipe, how much you like it on a scale of 0-10, the ingredients list, and the instructions for making it. Finally, just click 'Save Recipe' and you're done!
